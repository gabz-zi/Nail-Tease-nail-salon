Health & Hygiene

The health of our customers and hygiene is our utmost priority. That’s why all our tools are either single-use (and recycled) or sterilised in a hospital-grade autoclave machine between each use.

We also believe in ‘better-for-you’ products – all our polishes and gels are at least 3-free so you don’t need to worry about toxic chemicals.

The Nail Tease team are experts in their field, delivering world-class service all while adhering to industry-leading hygiene standards. Our highly trained nail artists are masters of their craft and will leave you feeling polished to perfection.
